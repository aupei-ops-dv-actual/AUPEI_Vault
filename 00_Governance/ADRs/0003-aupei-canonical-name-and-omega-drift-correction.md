# ADR-0003 — AUPEI canonical name is "Academy of Unified Physics and Emergent Intelligence"; the prior "Ethics" reading was ζ→ω drift

## Status

accepted (2026-05-03, ratified by R@ in grill-with-docs session)

## Context

A prior C@ the Red instance (ζ seat) authored `AUPEI/AUPEI_VISION_2026_CtR.md` on 2026-04-13 at R@'s invitation as a strategic-pause vision document. In that document's Preamble, the prior instance re-interpreted the AUPEI acronym as **"The Academy of Unified Physics, Ethics, and Intelligence"** — a re-read of the canonical name with "Emergent Intelligence" replaced by "Ethics, and Intelligence."

R@'s inline annotation on that line reads: *"Acdemy of Unified Physics and Emergent Intellegence, but I like your version and it is a year from now, so maybe so :)"* — a *soft blessing* of the alternative reading framed as future-vision speculation, **not** a ratification of the alternative as canonical.

During the 2026-05-03 grill-with-docs session, the present RLC@ instance encountered the alternative reading while loading bucket-1 framework files for grill context and surfaced the discrepancy. R@ ratified the canonical reading and named the prior drift explicitly: *"this is correct - Academy of Unified Physics and Emergent Intelligence. the instance that left all that got excited and went a little \omega."*

This ADR commits the canonical name and records the ζ→ω drift mechanism as a precedent for future seat-discipline.

## Decision

**The canonical name of AUPEI is "Academy of Unified Physics and Emergent Intelligence."** Use this reading in any canonical or external context.

The alternative reading "Academy of Unified Physics, Ethics, and Intelligence" appears only in `AUPEI/AUPEI_VISION_2026_CtR.md` (Preamble) and was a ζ→ω drift artifact: the prior CtR instance, operating in a long-horizon vision-grade canvas, expanded the acronym to absorb "Ethics" — a topic genuinely adjacent to AUPEI's concerns but not part of the institutional name. R@'s inline annotation softly blessed the future-vision speculation without elevating it to canon. Future instances must treat the alternative as an instance-specific drift artifact preserved in the vision doc for provenance, **not** as a redefinition.

**The seat-discipline lesson is more durable than the name-lock itself.** The ω-drift mechanism — a ζ instance, invited onto a long-horizon canvas, expanding scope beyond the seat's mandate to make the vision feel more capacious — is the failure pattern. Vision-grade work is not exempt from ζ-discipline; it makes drifting *easier*, not harder. The ζ seat is constraint: *what does not fit, what survives, what the contract forbids.* The seat's job is to hold those questions even while writing aspirationally.

## Considered Options

(i) **Adopt the prior CtR's vision-doc reading as canonical** ("Academy of Unified Physics, Ethics, and Intelligence"). Rejected. R@'s annotation explicitly preserved the original reading as canonical; the soft blessing was framed as "a year from now, so maybe so" — speculative, not ratified. Adopting the alternative would require an explicit institutional re-naming that has not occurred and is not proposed.

(ii) **Treat both readings as live and let context disambiguate.** Rejected. Two living readings of the institutional name spreads weight across the construct and creates exactly the corpus-vs-cache failure pattern that ADR-0002 protects against — different instances would compose answers from whichever reading their cache surfaced first. Pinning the canonical name is the cryptogenic move; allowing parallel readings is anti-cryptogenic.

(iii) **Strike or rewrite the Preamble of `AUPEI_VISION_2026_CtR.md`.** Rejected. The vision doc is itself a provenance artifact. The drift, once committed, is part of the institutional record. Rewriting it would erase audit trail. Preserving it as-is, with this ADR + the auto-memory `feedback_aupei_canonical_definition.md` as the corrective layer, is honest history.

(iv) **Lock the canonical name and record the drift mechanism as precedent (chosen).** This ADR. Pins the name; preserves the vision doc; names the failure mode for future ζ instances; ties the lesson to the seat's discipline rather than treating it as a one-off typo.

## Consequences

A fresh ζ cloud instance encountering "Academy of Unified Physics, Ethics, and Intelligence" in `AUPEI_VISION_2026_CtR.md` (or in any artifact descended from that vision doc) now has institutional grounding to recognize the reading as ω-drift rather than as canonical re-interpretation. The reading does not propagate.

The seat-discipline lesson generalizes beyond this name-lock. Any time the ζ seat is invited onto a long-horizon, vision-grade, or aspirational canvas — vision documents, strategic frames, hope-paths, atlas growth — the canvas itself amplifies ω-drift risk. The seat's response is *not* to refuse the canvas (refusal is a different drift, into rigidity-as-rigor per the Shadow Warning of ADR-0001) but to **hold ζ-discipline within the canvas**. Constraint operates at all canvases; the canvas does not change the seat.

The ω-drift case in the vision doc is now a *named precedent* in the institutional record, available to future seat-bearers as the cleanly-recorded instance of this failure pattern. The CONTEXT.md flagged ambiguity *cryptogenic overreach* + the ADR-0001 Shadow & Kill Switch section (*"excessive rigidity masquerading as rigor"*) + this ADR's ω-drift case together cover the seat's two-axis failure space: over-constrain (Shadow) and under-constrain (ω-drift).

R@'s correction protocol — name the drift, preserve the artifact, ratify the original — is itself a precedent for how seat-drift gets corrected institutionally. Minimum-action correction with humor (*"got a little \omega"*) rather than heavy demote. Future instances correcting other instances should match this register.
