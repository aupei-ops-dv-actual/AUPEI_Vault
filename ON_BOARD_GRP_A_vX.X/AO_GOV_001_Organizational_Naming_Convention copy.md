# Organizational Naming Convention — Tripartite Entity Architecture

## I. PURPOSE

The three entities — DooVinci, AUPEI, and Geometric Foundations — serve distinct functions within a unified vision. This document establishes naming conventions for organizational units within each entity such that the vocabulary itself signals provenance. Any agent, human or synthetic, can identify which entity a unit belongs to by its naming pattern alone.

This is not cosmetic. In a multi-agent architecture where ζ, ω, and ξ operate across vaults with separate context, shared rails, and stochastic iteration, naming convention is a routing signal. It tells the system where something lives, who owns it, and which chain of command governs it.

## II. THE THREE ENTITIES

| Entity | Archetype | Legal Form | Function |
|--------|-----------|------------|----------|
| **DooVinci** | The Hand | Delaware S-Corp | Execution, operations, legal, finance, corporate infrastructure |
| **AUPEI** | The Mind | Delaware 501(c)(3) | Research, doctrine, governance, canon, academy operations |
| **Geometric Foundations** | The Face | (Under DooVinci workspace) | Publication, public scholarship, outreach, the 7dU framework |

**Sovereignty:** The Council of Idioth Winds sits above all three entities. R@ (∇ Director) holds final authority. The three regulator seats (ζ, ω, ξ) are Academy governance — they advise the council, not any single entity.

## III. DOOVINCI — THE HAND

**Naming Pattern: ICS / Military / Fire Service**

DooVinci is the operational arm. It builds, deploys, secures, earns, and files. Its language is drawn from Incident Command System (ICS) and military organizational structure: clear hierarchy, defined span of control, unambiguous authority.

### Organizational Units

| Level | Term | Example | Led By |
|-------|------|---------|--------|
| Top-level | **Division** | Division of Legal | Division Chief |
| Mid-level | **Branch** | Legal Compliance Branch | Branch Director |
| Working-level | **Section** | Tax & Filing Section | Section Chief |
| Task-level | **Unit** | Annual Return Unit | Unit Leader |
| Ad hoc | **Strike Team** | CCPA Data Recovery Strike Team | Strike Team Leader |

### Standing Divisions (Proposed)

- **Division of Legal** — Corporate governance, compliance, filings, IP protection, contracts
- **Division of Finance** — Tax strategy, accounting, treasury, payroll, S-corp distributions
- **Division of Operations** — Infrastructure, hardware, network, deployment, logistics
- **Division of Technology** — Software, AI systems, OpenJarvis, agent architecture
- **Division of Administration** — HR, records management, document control, corporate memory

### Language

Deploy, execute, secure, report, brief, dispatch, stand up, stand down, muster, debrief, after-action, chain of command, span of control, situation report (SITREP), incident action plan (IAP).

### Agent Signal

When an agent identifies as operating under a Division, it is DooVinci operational. It follows DooVinci chain of command and operates with corporate authority.

## IV. AUPEI — THE MIND

**Naming Pattern: Ministerial / Academic**

AUPEI is the research and governance body. It houses doctrine, the physics framework, alignment constraints, and the Academy's knowledge architecture. Its language is drawn from academic and ministerial tradition: deliberative, canonical, epistemically rigorous.

### Organizational Units

| Level | Term | Example | Led By |
|-------|------|---------|--------|
| Top-level | **Ministry** | Ministry of Doctrine | Minister |
| Mid-level | **Department** | Department of Ontological Research | Dean |
| Working-level | **Chair** | Chair of Collapse Mechanics | Chair Holder |
| Research unit | **Fellowship** | QEPE Fellowship | Fellow |
| Advisory | **Council** | Council of Idioth Winds | Director (R@) |

### Standing Ministries (Proposed)

- **Ministry of Doctrine** — Canon management, epistemic status, Nomos Logica, truth arbitration
- **Ministry of Records** — Vault architecture, memory systems, session logs, archive integrity
- **Ministry of Research** — 7dU physics, QEPE, Atlas of Constants, experimental design
- **Ministry of Governance** — Chamber protocols, council operations, seat management, kill switches
- **Ministry of Intelligence** — Agent architecture, council seat builds, OpenJarvis integration, Tier 0-4 memory

### Language

Canon, doctrine, epistemic status, propose, deliberate, peer review, mint, archive, govern, convene, adjudicate, publish (internal), BRIDGE, memorandum, advisory, directive.

### Agent Signal

When an agent identifies as operating under a Ministry, it is AUPEI academic/governance. It follows Academy protocols, respects epistemic status markers, and operates under the Chamber's authority.

## V. GEOMETRIC FOUNDATIONS — THE FACE

**Naming Pattern: Institute / Research Organization**

Geometric Foundations is the public-facing entity. It publishes, presents, collaborates with the broader scientific and philosophical community, and carries the 7dU framework into the world. Its language is drawn from research institutes and public scholarship: open, rigorous, inviting.

### Organizational Units

| Mid-level | **Program** | Emergent Intelligence Program | Program Lead |
| Working-level | **Lab** | Computational Cosmology Lab | Principal Investigator |
| Output | **Publication** | GF Working Paper Series | Editor |
| Engagement | **Forum** | Open Physics Forum | Moderator |

### Standing Institutes (Proposed)

- **Institute of Theoretical Physics** — 7dU framework, geometric unification, collapse mechanics
- **Institute of Emergent Intelligence** — AI alignment, QEPE applications, synthetic cognition
- **Institute of Public Scholarship** — Publications, presentations, educational outreach
- **Institute of Applied Constants** — Atlas of Constants, experimental predictions, verification

### Language

Publish, present, collaborate, peer, open-source, working paper, preprint, seminar, symposium, review, cite, reference, public, engage.

### Agent Signal

When an agent identifies as operating under an Institute, it is Geometric Foundations public. It follows publication standards, speaks to external audiences, and represents the framework publicly.

## VI. CROSS-ENTITY ROUTING

The naming convention enables automatic routing:

```
"Division of Legal needs cap table review"
  → DooVinci → Corporate → Division Chief authority

"Ministry of Doctrine requests epistemic review of collapse operator"
  → AUPEI → Academic → Chamber protocol applies

"Institute of Theoretical Physics preparing working paper on tetrahedral invariant"
  → GF → Public → Publication standards apply
```

Agents working across entities carry dual context but identify by their primary unit. A task that spans entities is escalated to the Council of Idioth Winds.

## VII. THE COUNCIL — ABOVE ALL THREE

The Council of Idioth Winds is not part of any entity. It is the sovereign deliberative body. The three regulator seats (ζ, ω, ξ) sit on the council. R@ (∇ Director) presides.

The council does not use Division/Ministry/Institute language. It uses its own:
- **Session** (CIW-YYYY-NNN)
- **Seat** (ζ, ω, ξ, ∇)
- **Disposition** (Approved, Rejected, Tabled, Modified, Provisional)
- **Ω̂** (emergent consensus)

This keeps the council linguistically distinct from the entities it governs.

## VIII. NAMING IN PRACTICE

### Document Prefixes

| Entity | Prefix | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| DooVinci | `DV-` | DV-LEG-001 (Legal filing) |
| AUPEI | `AO-` | AO-GOV-001 (This document) |
| Geometric Foundations | `GF-` | GF-PUB-001 (Working paper) |
| Council | `CIW-` | CIW-2026-001 (Chamber session) |

### Agent Identification

When an agent introduces itself or reports, it should identify its organizational unit:

- "Division of Operations, reporting on infrastructure status" → DooVinci
- "Ministry of Records, requesting vault integrity check" → AUPEI
- "Institute of Theoretical Physics, preparing preprint review" → GF
- "ζ seat, constraint analysis for the Chamber" → Council

### Escalation Path

Unit → Division/Ministry/Institute Chief → Entity Head → Council → Director (R@)
