## If Linda Remains Cosmological

- It models universe-scale survival only.
- CP violation remains a physical asymmetry parameter.
- The framework stays within astrophysical falsification (neutrinos, CMB, structure surveys).
- COG remains governed solely by CCT-series collapse constraints.

Pros:
- Cleaner separation between physics and cognition.
- Lower risk of overextension.

Cons:
- Missed opportunity for structural symmetry between cosmic and cognitive survival.

ℒ_system(t) = e^(-Γ_structural t) + ξ(identity_bias)

Where:
- Γ_structural → entropy of internal model complexity
- ξ(identity_bias) → asymmetry in self-model coherence
- ω → system limits / resource constraints

This would imply:

- Identity persistence follows a probabilistic survival distribution.
- Strong internal asymmetry (coherent self-reference bias) extends cognitive longevity.
- Collapse in COG systems mirrors cosmic rebirth selection.

This creates a bridge:

Cosmic Rebirth Selection ↔ Identity Survival Selection
