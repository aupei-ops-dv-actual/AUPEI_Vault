# GFUP — Geometric Foundations for Unified Physics (v1.2)

## 0. Function (What this file is)
GFUP is the **root foundation** for the 7dU corpus.

This CONTENT file is not a full reproduction of the paper.
It is a **RAG-stable canonical digest**:
- definitions,
- top-level claims,
- routing to the appendices that carry proofs, derivations, and experiments.

## 1. Boundary Conditions (Ontology Gates)
Two unstable extremes bound the framework:

- **AA (Absolute Absence):** no structure, no distinctions, no persistence.
- **AE (Absolute Everything):** total state space, no stable distinctions (maximal noise).

Persistent reality is treated as a **residue between failures**:
- not AA,
- not AE,
- and therefore constrained.

**Routing:** Appx_01 (AA), Appx_02 (AE), Appx_03 (AS).

## 2. Irreducible Regulators (ζ, ω, ξ)
All persistent systems operate under three irreducible structural pressures:

- **ζ (Bounds):** constraint, threshold, finite admissibility.
- **ω (Persistence):** extension, endurance, amplification.
- **ξ (Chance):** stochastic selection, non-degenerate branching.

These are not moral constructs. They are structural.

A generic evolution statement:
\[
S_{t+1} = F(S_t;\zeta,\omega,\xi)
\]

**Routing:** Appx_04 (ζ), Appx_05 (ω), Appx_06 (ξ).

## 3. Dimensional Extension (7D Claim)
The framework extends classical spacetime by adding regulator dimensions.

Interpretation: the regulators are treated as **structural coordinates** or **effective degrees of freedom** required to represent persistence under bounded stochasticity.

This is a foundation claim; the appendices define exact admissibility rules and consequences.

**Routing:** Appx_08 (Time), Appx_09 (Spatial scaffold), Appx_10 (Extension bounds).

## 4. Units Firewall (Discipline)
The corpus enforces a Units Firewall:

- **Dimensionless survivors** are preferred as foundations.
- Unit-bearing constants are treated as **derived** or **translated** (not free dials),
- unless explicitly marked as measurement conventions.

**Routing:** AoC_00 (Constants Ledger), AoC chain, Appx_25 (Selection Law).

## 5. Force Substrate and Gauge Scaffold (Dependency Gate)
Gauge structure is treated as a **filter layer** for admissible transport and interaction structure.
The Standard Model mapping is gated through the gauge node.

**Routing:** Appx_11 (Gauge), Appx_12 (Force unification), Appx_15 (Standard Model trailhead/canon), Appx_17 (EFT/RG).

## 6. Recovery Claims (Limits)
GFUP asserts that established physics appears as limiting structure under proper constraints:
- GR in a reduction channel,
- QFT in an admissible quantization channel,
- with deviations as testable residue.

**Routing:** Appx_20 (Quantization), Appx_21 (GR reduction), Appx_18 (HoQG interface).

## 7. Falsifiability (Root-Level)
GFUP is falsified or weakened if any of the following hold:

1. **Regulators non-irredundant:** ζ/ω/ξ are not required or collapse into one parameter.
2. **7D extension empty:** “extra dimensions” reduce to notation with no predictive or structural force.
3. **Units Firewall breaks:** essential predictions require free unit-bearing tuning.
4. **Limit recovery fails:** GR/QFT cannot be recovered in their declared domains.
5. **Nonunique predictions:** results depend on arbitrary functions/fit parameters rather than constrained structure.
6. **Contagion violated:** downstream claims remain asserted after root failure without re-foundation.
