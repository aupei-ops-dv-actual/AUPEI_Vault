# Appx_00: The Collapse Map (Failure Topology & Dependency Spine)

## 0. The Rule of Propagation


[Image of a fault tree analysis diagram]

The 7dU Geometric Natural Selection framework is a strictly dependent architecture. Truth in this framework is not modular; it is emergent. Therefore, falsification is contagious. The Collapse Map defines the exact propagation paths of failure. If a parent node is falsified (the "Kill Switch" is tripped), all child nodes immediately collapse into unmotivated mathematics or interpretive narrative.

## 1. The Root Spine (Ontology & The Triad)
This is the absolute bedrock. If these fail, the universe does not emerge.

* **AA (Absolute Absence) & AE (Absolutely Everything)**
  * *Kill Switch:* If AA or AE are proven to be stable, persistent states rather than inherent paradoxes.
  * *Collapse Consequence:* Existence is no longer mandatory. The entire 7dU framework fails.
  * $\downarrow$ *Yields* $\downarrow$
* **The Operators ($\zeta, \omega, \xi$)**
  * *Kill Switch:* If constraint ($\zeta$), extension ($\omega$), and probability ($\xi$) can be reduced to one another, or if a fourth irreducible operator is required.
  * *Collapse Consequence:* The geometry of the 7dU is false. Book II collapses.

## 2. The Physical Spine (The Atlas of Constants)
Constants must emerge from the Root Spine. 

* **Appx_25 (The Selection Law)**
  * *Kill Switch:* If natural invariants cannot be generated via geometric selection (e.g., they require arbitrary inputs).
  * *Collapse Consequence:* Constants revert to anthropic tuning. 
  * $\downarrow$ *Yields* $\downarrow$
* **AoC_01 ($\pi$), AoC_02 ($c$), AoC_03 ($\alpha$)**
  * *Kill Switch:* If any of these are proven to be non-geometric or independently varying.
  * *Collapse Consequence:* Spatial closure, causality, and volumetric scaling fail. Standard Model integration (Appx_11, Appx_12) collapses.

## 3. The Cosmological Spine (Gravity & Rebirth)
Scales the Physical Spine to the macroscopic universe.

* **Appx_18 (Hypothesis of Quantum Gravity - HoQG)**
  * *Kill Switch:* If quantum fluctuations ($\xi$) are proven independent of spatial constraint ($\zeta$) and extension ($\omega$).
  * *Collapse Consequence:* Time does not emerge. The framework reverts to classical GR overlay. 
  * $\downarrow$ *Yields* $\downarrow$
* **Appx_24 (The Linda Function)** & **Appx_27a (Gravity Threshold)**
  * *Kill Switch:* If universal decay is purely deterministic (Linda fails), or if dark matter is found to be particulate (Gravity Threshold fails).
  * *Collapse Consequence:* 7dU loses its explanation for stability and galactic rotation. Dark acceleration (Appx_14) collapses.

## 4. The Cognitive Spine (Agency & Observers)
The emergence of the observer from the geometry.

* **COG_00 / Appx_23 (CCT-1 Identity)**
  * *Kill Switch:* If identity and awareness are not bounded by the $\zeta/\xi$ paradox limit.
  * *Collapse Consequence:* Observer mechanics decouple from physics.
  * $\downarrow$ *Yields* $\downarrow$
* **Appx_26 (Nomos Logica)**
  * *Kill Switch:* If the thermodynamic survival ethos (Grace, Temperance, Fortitude, Justice) contradicts the geometric operators.
  * *Collapse Consequence:* The Academy architecture fails; ethical alignment becomes subjective dogma.

## 5. The Falsification Layer (The Guillotine)
* **Appx_27 (Proposed Experiments)**
  * *Role:* The active boundary. This is where the physical universe is invited to strike the kill switches outlined above. If the Ledger (Appx_27) ceases to produce testable discriminators, the entire Collapse Map freezes, and 7dU dies as a scientific theory.
