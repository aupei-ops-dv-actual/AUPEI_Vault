## Letter (Send to: dsar@openai.com and privacy@openai.com)

**Subject: Formal CCPA Subject Access Request — Account [YOUR ACCOUNT EMAIL]**

To Whom It May Concern:

I am writing to exercise my rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.100 et seq. I am a California resident and a paying subscriber to OpenAI's services. This is a formal request to access my personal information pursuant to CCPA § 1798.110.

**I request that OpenAI provide me with:**

1. The complete contents of all ChatGPT conversations associated with my account, including all message text, timestamps, metadata, conversation titles, and project/folder assignments.

2. All personal information OpenAI has collected about me, including but not limited to: usage data, account information, billing records, and any derived or inferred data.

3. The categories of sources from which my personal information was collected.

4. The business or commercial purpose for collecting my personal information.

**Account identification:**

- Account email: [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS]
- Account type: Business (individual)
- Subscription: [YOUR PLAN NAME]
- I completed identity verification on March 11, 2026 (photo + government-issued ID), which was confirmed as passed.

**Context:**

I have attempted to obtain my data through OpenAI's standard self-service export (Settings → Data Controls → Export Data). My export requests have been automatically cancelled. I believe this is due to a system error that classifies my individual business account as an Enterprise account, routing my requests through an incorrect workflow that auto-rejects them.

I have filed a support ticket regarding this issue and have not received a resolution.

**I want to be clear:** I am not requesting deletion. I am requesting access to and a copy of my data — specifically, the full archive of my ChatGPT conversation history, which spans approximately three years of use.

**Legal obligations:**

Under CCPA § 1798.110, OpenAI is required to respond to this request within 45 calendar days of receipt. If additional time is needed, OpenAI must notify me within the initial 45-day period and may extend the response period by an additional 45 days. This request may not be refused on the basis of an internal account classification error.

Under CCPA § 1798.150, a business that fails to comply with a consumer's access request may be subject to civil penalties.

I am prepared to verify my identity through any reasonable means OpenAI requires. I have already completed photo and government ID verification through your system.

Please confirm receipt of this request and provide an expected timeline for fulfillment.

## Notes

- **Send to both addresses:** dsar@openai.com is their dedicated data subject access request inbox. privacy@openai.com is the general privacy team. Send to both.
- **CC yourself** so you have a timestamped record.
- **Save a PDF** of the sent email for your records.
- **45-day clock** starts on the date they receive the email.
- **If no response in 45 days:** You can file a complaint with the California Attorney General's office at oag.ca.gov/privacy.
- **Keep the support ticket open** — reference it in the email if you have a ticket number.
- **Do not accept** a partial export or a "we'll get back to you" without a specific date.
