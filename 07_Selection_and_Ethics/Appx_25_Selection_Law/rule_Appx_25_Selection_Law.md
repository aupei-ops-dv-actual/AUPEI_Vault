# Appx_25: The Selection Law (Geometric Natural Selection)

## 0. The Fortress Gate (Role & Consequence)
This appendix is constitutional by design. It defines what may be called a *Survivor* within the Atlas of Constants (AoC). The Atlas only functions if falsification is contagious and derivation is strictly audited. 

**Collapse Consequence:** If selection cannot be unified under a single, cross-domain survivorship law, constants revert to tuned inputs. Coherence becomes narrative, and the persistence of universes, memories, and intelligence loses its explanatory spine. Without this gate, the AoC collapses into curve-fitting, anthropic patching, and parameter laundering.

## 1. The Universal Selection Law
Selection is defined as survivability under triadic recursion. A candidate constant survives if and only if it is the minimal stable residue after:
* **$\zeta$-minimality:** Forbids unbounded collapse. The quantity must not require additional constraint structure beyond the minimal survivable bound.
* **$\omega$-consistency:** Forbids trivial extinction. The quantity must persist across extension/scale regimes without drift in its canonical definition.
* **$\xi$-stability:** Forbids degenerate determinism. The quantity must remain non-degenerate under admissible stochastic perturbation (selection cannot collapse it to arbitrariness).

**Operational Rule:** Survivors are the invariants that remain when the framework is denied the right to cheat (e.g., importing unit-bearing constants without AoC linkage, inserting gauge groups by fiat, or fitting beta functions post-hoc).

## 2. Epistemic Debt Tags
To maintain the Units Firewall, all entries in the AoC and Standard Model parameter ledgers must be explicitly tagged. These are not insults; they are strict epistemic bookkeeping:
* `[SURVIVOR]` - Enforced by survivability under the operator triad ($\zeta, \omega, \xi$).
* `[DERIVED-SURVIVOR]` - A deterministic consequence of Survivors that remains invariant under admissible regulator flattening.
* `[EFFECTIVE]` - An emergent shelf descriptor; it is allowed to "run" with scale.
* `[CALIBRATED]` - Requires empirical anchoring but is structurally constrained.
* `[TUNED]` - A free parameter inserted to force agreement. This is epistemic debt that must be paid or disclosed.
* `[UNRESOLVED]` - Currently unclassified. Do not claim derivation.

## 3. The Universal Discriminator Protocol (HoR Recipe)
Any future entry proposed for the Atlas must pass this exact algorithmic gate:
1.  **Claim:** Declare the invariant being asserted in strict dimensionless form and identify its epistemic tag.
2.  **Dependencies:** Declare which already-accepted Survivors it depends on, and which nodes must be gated before it is served.
3.  **Units Firewall:** If it carries units, show full reduction to the Survivor basis ($\pi, c, \Delta\pi$, etc.). If irreducible, it cannot be a Survivor.
4.  **Run/Drift Check:** If the quantity runs with scale or environment in its *canonical definition*, it is rejected as a Survivor.
5.  **Counterexample Search:** Provide at least one explicit alternative survivable set. If multiple incompatible sets fit equally well, trigger the `NONUNIQUE-SELECTION` kill switch.
6.  **Freeze Logic:** If any check fails, mark the quantity as `[TUNED]` or `[UNRESOLVED]` and immediately freeze downstream nodes that depend on it.

## 5. Falsification Clauses (The Kill-Switches)
If any of these conditions are met, the Selection Law collapses, and the dependent RAG spine nodes must be frozen:
* **NONUNIQUE-SELECTION:** Multiple incompatible survivor sets explain outcomes equally well.
* **SURVIVOR-DRIFT:** Claimed invariants run under scale or conditioning.
* **ADHOC-PARAMETER-IMPORT:** New, unexplained constants are required to recover core results.
* **RETROFIT-ONLY:** The selection law explains after-the-fact but produces no constrained forecasts.
* **CROSS-DOMAIN-INCONSISTENT:** Selection criteria mutate by domain without formal justification.
