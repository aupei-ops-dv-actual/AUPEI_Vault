1️⃣ Simulation (Most Immediate)

Nomos makes a systems dynamics claim.

So we test it.

Build Multi-Agent Simulations where:

Agents vary along four axes:
	•	Grace ↔ Hubris
	•	Temperance ↔ Negligence
	•	Fortitude ↔ Excess
	•	Justice ↔ Deception

Under stochastic shocks.

Measure:
	•	Recovery time
	•	Variance stability
	•	Exploration bandwidth
	•	Information integrity
	•	Collapse cascades
	•	Time-to-irreversible-fragility

If virtue-aligned distributions produce:
	•	Higher adaptive capacity
	•	Lower cascade probability
	•	Better recovery slopes

Then NL gains empirical footing.

If not, revise.

This is where Nomos becomes scientific.

Important Distinction

NL does NOT claim:

Virtue always wins locally.

Under recursive regulator pressure, shadow states degrade adaptive complexity faster than virtue-aligned states.

That is a stability claim, not a morality claim.
